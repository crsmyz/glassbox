Developers

Legislation tracking for the City of Philadelphia!

Glassbox is an app that’s all about tracking legislation moving through City Council in Philadelphia. It allows you to browse all of the legislation being proposed and considered in City Council through a feed which gives Bill titles, summaries, sponsors, current status and a fully readable Bill. This helps the people of Philadelphia to get a better look into how City Council is making laws to better their lives and the city.
